# Custom Models

Pre-trained AI models by Kolbo.AI for specific visual styles.

## What Are Custom Models?

Custom Models are pre-trained by the Kolbo team for specific aesthetic styles:
- iPhone photo style
- Storyboard illustrations  
- Vintage 2000s photography
- And more styles

## How to Use

1. Go to Text to Image or Training Lab
2. Select a Custom Model from the model selector
3. Write your prompt
4. Generate

The AI will create images matching that specific style.

## Benefits

- **No Training Needed**: Pre-trained and ready to use
- **Consistent Style**: Reliable, predictable aesthetic
- **Quality**: Professionally trained models
- **Time-Saving**: Get specific styles instantly

## Available Styles

Custom Models cover various aesthetics:
- Photography styles
- Illustration styles
- Artistic styles
- Era-specific looks
- Genre-specific aesthetics

Check the model selector for the current list of available Custom Models.

## Difference from Training Lab

**Custom Models:**
- Pre-trained by Kolbo
- Available to all users
- Specific aesthetic styles
- Ready to use immediately

**Training Lab:**
- You train your own models
- On your own images
- For your specific character/style/product
- Requires training time
