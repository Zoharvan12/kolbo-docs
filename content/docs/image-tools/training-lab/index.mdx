# Training Lab

Train custom AI models on your own characters, styles, or products.

## What It Does

Training Lab lets you:
- Train models on your uploaded images
- Create custom character models
- Develop unique style models
- Generate new images using your trained models

## Training Process

1. Upload training images (10-30 images recommended)
2. Choose base model
3. Configure training settings
4. Start training (takes time depending on settings)
5. Use your trained model for generation

## Generation with Trained Models

Once trained, you can:
- Generate with your custom model using any base model
- Combine multiple trained models
- Use reference images alongside trained models
- Apply inpainting with your character/style
- Use image prompts for better control

## Features

**Base Models:**
Choose from available base models for training

**Model Combinations:**
Combine multiple trained models in one generation

**Advanced Tools:**
- Reference images
- Inpainting
- Image prompts
- Style control

**Model Library:**
- Import models from CivitAI
- Share trained models
- Organize your models

## Use Cases

**Character Consistency:**
Train on a character to generate them in different scenes

**Brand Style:**
Train on brand visuals for consistent marketing content

**Product Integration:**
Train on products to show them in various contexts

**Artistic Style:**
Train on an art style to apply it to new images
