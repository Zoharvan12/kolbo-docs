# Creative Director

The Creative Director is Kolbo.AI's most powerful tool for generating complete creative sets and variations in one command.

## Overview

Instead of generating images one by one, Creative Director creates:
- Complete product photography sets
- Character variations across different scenes
- Marketing campaign visuals
- Consistent brand imagery
- Multiple angles and contexts

## Key Features

### Complete Creative Sets
Generate entire sets of related images:
- Product photos in different settings
- Character in various poses and scenarios
- Marketing materials for campaigns
- Branded content variations

### Smart Variations
Intelligent variation system:
- Maintain consistency across images
- Apply different styles automatically
- Change contexts while preserving subject
- Create A/B testing variations

### Templates
Pre-built templates for common use cases:
- Product photography templates
- Character design templates
- Marketing campaign templates
- Social media content templates

### Text & Image Prompting
Flexible input options:
- Text descriptions
- Reference images
- Combination of text + images
- Style references

## Use Cases

- **E-commerce**: Generate complete product photography sets
- **Character Design**: Create character in multiple scenarios
- **Marketing**: Build campaign assets quickly
- **Branding**: Maintain visual consistency
- **Social Media**: Create content variations
