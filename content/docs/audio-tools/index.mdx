# Audio Tools

Kolbo.AI provides professional audio creation tools for music, speech, voice manipulation, and sound effects.

## Overview

The Audio Tools suite includes:
- **Music Generation**: Create music with Suno AI
- **Text to Speech**: Convert text to natural voice
- **Voice to Voice**: Transform and clone voices
- **Speech to Text**: Transcribe audio and video
- **Text to Sound**: Generate sound effects

## Available Tools

### Music Generation (Suno)
Create original music from text descriptions:
- Any genre and style
- Instrumental or with lyrics
- Custom duration
- Professional quality
- Commercial use rights

### Text to Speech
Natural-sounding voice generation:
- **Eleven Labs**: Premium voice quality
- **DeepDub**: Professional dubbing voices
- Multiple languages and accents
- Emotional control
- Voice customization

### Voice to Voice
Transform and manipulate voices:
- Voice transformation
- Age, gender, accent changes
- Voice cloning from samples
- Character voices
- Brand voice consistency

### Speech to Text
Accurate transcription:
- Audio file transcription
- Video transcription
- Multiple languages
- Timestamp support
- Subtitle generation
- Export formats (SRT, VTT, TXT)

### Text to Sound
Generate sound effects:
- Describe any sound in text
- Environmental sounds
- Action sounds
- Object sounds
- Custom audio effects

## Best Practices

**Music Generation:**
- Be specific about genre and mood
- Include instruments and tempo
- Specify vocal style if needed
- Generate variations for options

**Text to Speech:**
- Choose appropriate voice for content
- Consider accent and language
- Match voice to brand identity
- Test different voices

**Voice Cloning:**
- Use high-quality voice samples
- Record in quiet environment
- Consistent tone in samples
- Multiple samples for best results

**Transcription:**
- Use clear audio for best accuracy
- Specify correct language
- Review and edit if needed
- Export in appropriate format

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Quality Input**: Clear audio/text for best output
2. **Right Tool**: Choose the tool for your specific need
3. **Experiment**: Try different voices and settings
4. **Iterate**: Generate variations
5. **Combine Tools**: Use with video and image tools
6. **Professional Polish**: Edit and enhance as needed
7. **Test**: Preview before final export
