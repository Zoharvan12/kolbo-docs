Create talking videos with automatic lip synchronization. Animate characters or dub videos in different languages.

## Two Modes

**Image-to-Video**: Animate character images with audio
- Upload character image + audio
- AI creates talking character video
- Perfect for avatars, explainers, social content

**Video-to-Video**: Change language/dubbing in existing videos
- Upload video + new audio (different language/voice)
- AI adjusts lip movements to match
- Perfect for multilingual content, translations, dubbing

## Audio Options

- **Upload Audio**: MP3, WAV, etc.
- **Generate Audio**: Text-to-speech (Eleven Labs, DeepDub, 40+ languages)
- **Voice Cloning**: Clone and use custom voices

## How to Use

### Image-to-Video
1. Upload character face image (front-facing, high-res)
2. Add audio (upload, generate, or voice clone)
3. Generate - AI animates with perfect lip sync
4. Export (upscale if needed)

### Video-to-Video
1. Upload source video (clear face visibility)
2. Add new audio (different language/voice)
3. Generate - AI adjusts lip movements
4. Export

## Use Cases

**Content Creation**: YouTube, social media, educational videos
**Marketing**: Product demos, brand mascots, multilingual campaigns
**Localization**: Translate and dub for different markets
**Business**: Training videos, presentations

## Best Practices

1. Use front-facing, well-lit images/videos
2. Clear audio with minimal background noise
3. Appropriate pace (not too fast)
4. Test short clips first
5. Upscale for final quality

Works best with front-facing subjects. Audio quality affects lip sync accuracy.
