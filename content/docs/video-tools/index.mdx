# Video Tools

Kolbo.AI provides powerful video creation and transformation tools with multiple AI models and unique features.

## Overview

The Video Tools suite includes:
- **Image to Video**: Animate images with multiple modes
- **Text to Video**: Generate videos from text descriptions
- **Video to Video**: Transform existing videos
- **Lipsync**: AI-powered video dubbing and character animation

## Available AI Models

- **Kling**: High-quality video generation with presets
- **Hailuo 2.3**: New advanced video model
- **Seedance**: Dynamic video generation
- **Grok Imagine**: Video generation from X.ai
- **Sora 2**: Cinematic video generation (with presets)
- **Veo 3**: Advanced video model

## Tool Comparison

### Image to Video
Animate static images:
- Single image mode
- First and last frame mode
- Elements mode (animate multiple elements)
- Draw to video mode
- Best for bringing images to life

### Text to Video
Create videos from descriptions:
- Pure text-to-video generation
- Video presets (special effects, camera movements)
- Cinematic presets with Sora 2/Veo 3
- Best for creating original video content

### Video to Video
Transform existing videos:
- Style transfer for videos
- Motion transfer to characters
- Character replacement
- Best for video transformations

### Lipsync
AI-powered dubbing:
- Image-to-video with audio (animate characters)
- Video-to-video dubbing (change language/voice)
- Automatic lip synchronization
- Best for character animation and multilingual content

## Video Editing Features

Available across all video tools:
- **Upscale to 4K**: Enhance video resolution
- **Reframe**: Change aspect ratio intelligently
- **Remove Background**: Automatic background removal
- **Audio Generation**: Add AI-generated audio/music

## Special Features

### Video Presets
Pre-configured effects and movements:
- Special effects (explosions, transformations, etc.)
- Camera movements (zoom, pan, orbit)
- Crazy situations (viral-worthy scenarios)

### Sora 2 Presets
Cinematic video generation:
- Movie trailers
- Advertisement styles
- Dramatic scenarios
- Professional cinematic looks

### Elements Mode
Unique multi-element animation:
- Add multiple elements to a scene
- Animate each element independently
- Combine elements in one video
- Create complex animations

### Character Replace
Swap characters in videos:
- Replace any character with another
- Maintain original motion
- Seamless integration

### Motion Transfer
Animate using reference videos:
- Apply video motion to character images
- Transfer dance moves, actions, gestures
- Create consistent character animations

## Which Tool Should I Use?

**Use Image to Video when:**
- You have static images to animate
- Creating product demos from photos
- Bringing character art to life
- Adding motion to stills

**Use Text to Video when:**
- Creating original video content
- You have a clear vision/script
- Using presets for effects
- Generating cinematic content

**Use Video to Video when:**
- Transforming existing videos
- Transferring motion to characters
- Replacing characters
- Applying style changes to video

**Use Lipsync when:**
- Animating characters with dialogue
- Multilingual dubbing
- Character talking videos
- Synchronizing audio with video
