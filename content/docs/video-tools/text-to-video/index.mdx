Create videos from text descriptions - no images or videos required.

## AI Models

- **Kling**: High-quality, versatile
- **Hailuo 2.3**: Advanced realism
- **Seedance**: Dynamic, energetic
- **Sora 2**: Cinematic, professional
- **Veo 3**: Cutting-edge generation

## Presets

**Special Effects**: Explosions, transformations, weather, magical effects
**Camera Movements**: Zoom, pan, orbit, tracking, crane
**Crazy Situations**: Viral scenarios, unexpected combinations
**Sora 2/Veo 3 Presets**: Movie trailers, advertisements, cinematic styles

## How to Use

1. **Write Prompt**: Describe subject, action, environment, camera movement, mood
2. **Choose Model & Presets**: Select model (or use Smart Agent) + apply presets
3. **Set Parameters**: Duration (2-10s), aspect ratio, quality
4. **Generate & Refine**: Review, regenerate with adjustments

## Best Practices

**Effective Prompts:**
- Be specific and detailed
- Describe motion and action
- Include camera movement
- Specify mood and lighting

**Model Selection:**
- Kling: General purpose
- Hailuo 2.3: Realistic scenes
- Seedance: Dynamic action
- Sora 2/Veo 3: Cinematic quality

**Tips for Cinematic Results:**
1. Use Sora 2/Veo 3 for professional quality
2. Apply cinematic presets
3. Specify camera movements
4. Include lighting and atmosphere details
5. Generate multiple variations
6. Upscale for final quality
