# Use Case Examples

Explore real-world applications of Kolbo.AI across different industries and use cases.

## Overview

This guide provides practical examples of how different professionals and businesses use Kolbo.AI to solve real problems and create value.

## By Industry

### Marketing & Advertising

#### Social Media Marketing Campaign
**Challenge**: Create consistent, engaging content across multiple platforms

**Solution:**
1. **Planning** (AI Board)
   - Campaign mood board
   - Content calendar
   - Platform strategy

2. **Asset Creation**
   - Hero images (Creative Director)
   - Platform variations (multiple aspect ratios)
   - Video content (Image to Video + Lipsync)
   - Carousel posts

3. **Execution**
   - Export optimized for each platform
   - Batch download
   - Schedule posts

**Results:**
- 30 platform-optimized posts
- 5 short-form videos
- 10 carousel graphics
- Completed in 3 hours vs. 2 days

#### Product Launch Campaign
**Challenge**: Launch new product with complete marketing assets

**Workflow:**
1. Train model on product (Training Lab)
2. Generate product in various contexts (lifestyle, studio, action)
3. Create video demonstrations
4. Generate email graphics
5. Create social content
6. Build landing page mockups (Artifacts)

**Deliverables:**
- 50+ product images
- 10 video variations
- Email campaign graphics
- Social media assets
- Website mockups

**Challenge**: Manage multiple client projects efficiently

**Structure:**
```
Organization/
├── Client A/
│   ├── Brand Assets/
│   ├── Campaign 1/
│   └── Campaign 2/
├── Client B/
└── Internal/
```

**Per-Client Workflow:**
1. Train models on client brand
2. Create client-specific templates
3. Set up approval workflows
